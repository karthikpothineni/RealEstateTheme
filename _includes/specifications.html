Teak wood frames having good quality of flush doors with hardware fittings for both main and internal doors

Reputed make UPVC sliding windows fitted with iron grills and glass

Granite top platform and stainless steel sink. Provision for fixing water purifier, exhaust fan and chimney

Putty for internal walls, exterior emulsion for external wall, plastic emulsion for ceiling and enamel paint(2 coatings) for doors

Concealed copper wiring in modular switches.Adequate fan, tv, telephone points and 15A/5A power plugs

Black top roads as per R&B specifications/CC roads

Planting trees along the road sides covering the entire avenue

Water

Municipal water provision, water sump and water tank up to 2000 litres capacity to individual house
